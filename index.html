Entrepreneur's Guide

Objective: Achieve the highest Net Worth (Cash + Asset Value - Debt) at the end of the day limit.

1. Game Grid & Map: The town is a grid. Click on properties (like Grocery, Restaurant, Mechanic, Farm, Bank, AdServices) to inspect details, buy, or adjust settings. Roads are for paths, houses generate customers.

2. B2C pricing: Owners set prices. If prices are high relative to town affluence, customers will avoid you. Upgrades and ad campaigns increase customer willingness to visit.

3. Supply Chain: Groceries and Restaurants require raw goods. Buy them wholesale from a player-owned Farm. If you have no raw goods, you pay expensive emergency import costs ($18 for Grocery, $28 for Restaurant) for each customer!

4. Ad Campaigns: Buy campaigns from AdServices to spike advertising awareness. If player-owned, the transaction fee goes to the AdServices owner.

5. Bank Services: Take loans or repay debt. Loans accrue daily interest. If a player owns the bank, all loan interest goes to their cash balance! Warning: Unpaid debt at the end of the game incurs a 20% penalty.

6. Skills Upgrade: Spend cash to level up 5 key skills (Technology, Social, Planning, Marketing, Management) to receive powerful operational discounts and bonuses.
